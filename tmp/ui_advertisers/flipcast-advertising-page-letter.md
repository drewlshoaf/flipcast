# Letter to the Dev Team

Team,

We need to add a dedicated **Advertising** page to the Flipcast marketing site. This page should feel fully consistent with the current Flipcast brand direction we have been developing: light background, playful but polished gradients, and a modern mix of pink, blue, and green accents. It should not read like a legal or corporate information page. It needs to feel like part of the product story.

This page will be reached from one of the **small footer links** labeled **Advertising**. That means two things matter:

1. The footer link itself needs to be present and visually subtle.
2. The destination page still needs to feel premium, intentional, and conversion-oriented.

The purpose of the page is to help advertisers understand why Flipcast is a compelling ad channel and to give them a clear way to raise their hand for more information. It should communicate our advertising program, our reach, our unique positioning, and our targeted advertising approach. The page is not meant to be a deep analytics portal or a full media kit download center yet. It is a polished interest-generation page.

The tone should be confident, modern, and strategic. We are presenting Flipcast as a differentiated audio platform: personalized, on-demand, topic-driven, and better aligned to listener intent than generic broad inventory. The visual language should support that message with soft depth, glow, rounded cards, and a high-end startup feel.

From a UX standpoint, I want a page with a strong hero, supporting information blocks, a section on targeted/contextual advertising, and a clear advertiser-interest call to action. This should feel like a landing page, not a documentation page.

Please make sure the page includes:
- a strong hero statement
- a short explanation of the advertising program
- messaging about reach and high-intent listening
- messaging about unique positioning
- messaging about contextual or targeted advertising
- a clear contact or advertiser-interest form area
- the small footer link labeled **Advertising** pointing to `/advertising`

This page should match the existing site theme and feel visually coherent with the home page and other polished marketing pages we’ve been exploring.

Thanks,
Andrew
